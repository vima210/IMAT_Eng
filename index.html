Estimated 2024 IMAT Ranking

Insert your score and you'll get an estimate of your position in the EU 10th October Ranking. The number you'll see is the projected number of people in front of you.





Below you will find an ESTIMATE of the last available positions for each university. The first call will be on October 10th. The last call is an estimate of the final opportunity for admission after all the rounds. The optimistic estimate is based on 2022 data, while the pessimistic one is based on 2023. If your score is lower than the last available position, you are in (green box), but if it is higher, well, let's hope the model is wrong (red box)!
